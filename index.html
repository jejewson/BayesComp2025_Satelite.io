Bayesian Computation and Inference with Misspecified models
National University of Singapore, 16-17 June 2025

This Bayes Comp 2025 Satellite Workshop is about methods, theory and applications for Bayesian inference with misspecified models.

Call for Poster Sessions Abstracts

We would like to invite the submisison of abstracts for the poster session on the second afternoon. Abstracts can be submitted here and should be limited to 200 words. The deadline for abstract submission is 1st June 2025. A selection of accepted postes will also have the oppourtunity to give a spotlight presentation before the poster session.
